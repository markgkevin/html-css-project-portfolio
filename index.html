About Us

Located in the heart of Madison, Wisconsin, Hair Day stands as a premier boutique hair salon, renowned for its expertise in cut, color, and styling. With a commitment to excellence, this establishment goes beyond traditional services, offering exceptional makeup and nail treatments to pamper clients from head to toe. What truly sets Hair Day apart is its warm and inviting atmosphere, designed to create a haven of relaxation and rejuvenation. Embracing the avant-garde, this salon is celebrated for its fearless exploration of edgy hair colors, pushing the boundaries of self-expression. Moreover, their team of skilled professionals stays updated with the latest trends, ensuring clients receive the most fashionable and on-point haircuts available. At Hair Day, the art of transformation and the pursuit of beauty come together harmoniously.
